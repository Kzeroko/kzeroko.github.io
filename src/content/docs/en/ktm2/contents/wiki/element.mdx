# Elemental Weaknesses

Elemental weakness is a mechanic in KTM2. In KTM2, creatures may have different elements, or multiple different elements.

Different types of attacks have damage multipliers and reductions against creatures of different elements.

---

## Damage Calculation

If you use a very effective damage attack to counter an element, you will get a +25% damage bonus.

If you use a less effective damage attack to counter an element, you will get a -25% damage reduction.

Multiple elements:

If there are more than 2 categories of effective elemental damage, an additional 10% bonus is added for each layer.

✎Example:
```
Assuming you deal effective elemental damage to a creature with Fire, Lightning, and Earth elements at the same time, you will deal 125% + (2 * 10%) = 145% damage.
The same applies to ineffective elemental attacks, which will eventually deal 75% - (2 * 10%) = 55% damage.
```
---

## Damage Relationships

<br />

【Normal Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Astral

❌ Ineffective damage against it: None

---

【Dragon Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Dragon

❌ Ineffective damage against it: All

---

【Shard Element / Armor Type】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Soul, Blast

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Trident, Thorns, Melee, Earth

---

【Arcane Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Arcane, Astral

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Non-magical magic (e.g. poison), melee-type spells

---

【Fire Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Water, Frost

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Fire, Flame, Wind, Blast

---

【Water Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Frost, Lightning

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Fire, Flame, Water, Melee

---

【Frost Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Melee, Execute, Fire, Flame

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Lightning, Frost, Wind, Water

---

【Wind Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Fire, Flame

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Thorns, Lightning, Melee

---

【Earth Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Blast, Earth

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Trident, Thorns, Melee, Normal, Wind, Lightning

---

【Soul Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Light, Dark, Heal

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Soul, Execute, Melee, Normal, Thorns, Astral

---

【Astral Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Soul

❌ Ineffective damage against it: Light, Dark, Arcane

---

【Light Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Dark, Wither

❌ Ineffective damage against it: All

---

【Dark Element】

✅ Very effective damage against it: Light, Heal

❌ Ineffective damage against it: All

---

P.S. Unmentioned will cause normal damage, not the final version, may be changed later.
