# What is "Comfort"?

"Comfort" is a mechanic in KTM2. In KTM2, comfort is a visible value bound to the player.

Comfort changes with factors such as environment, combat, diet, and sleep, and in turn affects movement speed, digging speed, and hunger consumption speed.

---

## 📌Comfort Stages

Comfort is divided into three stages, each with different effects:

| Stage | Value Range | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Pain | 0 ~ 40 | Tired and uncomfortable, in a low state |
| Normal | 41 ~ 70 | Normal state, no obvious positive or negative effects |
| Relax | 71 ~ 100 | Relaxed body and mind, more comfortable in action |

---

## 📌Natural Change of Comfort

### Basic Natural Decay

Under normal circumstances, as long as the comfort is greater than 0, the comfort will be settled once in a while.

Without being completely offset by the environment, comfort naturally decreases by 1 point approximately every 60 seconds.

### Cases where Comfort is "Paused"

In the following states, the player's comfort is completely frozen and will not change naturally:

- Creative Mode / Spectator Mode
- Sleeping in bed
- Having the comfort effect of Farmer's Delight

### The effect of "Nourishment" on the decay rate

If the player has the nourishment effect of Farmer's Delight, the comfort settlement cycle will become 1.5 times the original (becomes once every 90 seconds).

### The effect of the environment on comfort

Each time comfort is settled, in addition to the basic -1 point, an additional correction value will be obtained according to the surrounding environment. The final environmental correction value will be limited to between -5 and +1 (in the best case, comfort does not drop, and in the worst case, comfort drops by 6 points at a time).

**When idle at home, comfort no longer drops**

- Within 16 blocks of the "respawn point" or "world spawn point"
- And the head is covered

When the above conditions are met: environmental correction +1

---

**It's more uncomfortable to be in the rain without a helmet**

- The world is raining
- Open air
- The player is not wearing a helmet

When the above conditions are met: environmental correction an additional -1

---

**It's more tormenting to not wear a full set of armor in the cold snow**

- The precipitation type of the biome is snow
- The player is not wearing a full set of armor

When the above conditions are met: environmental correction an additional -1

---

**The environment in the Nether will give more pressure**

- The dimension is the Nether

When the above conditions are met:

- When wearing a full set of armor, the environmental correction is an additional -1
- When the armor is incomplete, the environmental correction is an additional -3

## 📌The effect of food on comfort

Eating is one of the main ways to increase comfort. Each time you eat, the change in comfort is calculated according to the following formula:

> Final comfort change = basic comfort of food × quality bonus × diversity bonus

Basic comfort of food: Each food has a default basic comfort configuration. Some bad foods have a negative basic comfort by default.

Quality bonus: The food refinement bonus for the chef profession. The better the food, the higher the positive comfort bonus. Even for negative foods, good quality can reduce the discomfort penalty.

Diversity bonus: The more you eat the same food, the worse the comfort recovery effect. Encouraging a diversified diet will result in a higher comfort gain.

The diversity logic will record the last 6 kinds of food the player has eaten, and then determine the diversity coefficient according to the number of times this food has appeared in the last 6 times:

| Number of appearances in the last 6 meals | Diversity factor |
|---|---|
| 0 times | 1.0 (100%) |
| 1 time | 0.7 (70%) |
| 2 times | 0.4 (40%) |
| ≥3 times | 0.1 (10%) |

The diversity factor only works for positive comfort changes, while negative changes are fully effective.

---

## 📌Sleep and Comfort Recovery

When the player sleeps in bed and skips the night, a sleep recovery effect is settled.

Recovers up to 30 points of comfort (recovers 70% of the missing value, but does not directly fill up comfort).

---

## 📌The effect of combat and injury on comfort

Whenever a player takes damage, comfort is reduced based on the damage value.

- The higher the damage, the more comfort is deducted.
- The higher the proportion of damage to maximum health, the more comfort is deducted.
- The deduction value has a diminishing protection, and there will be no sudden large deductions.
- A maximum of 20 points of comfort can be deducted at a time, and a minimum of 1 point.

---

## 📌The effect of comfort on movement speed and digging speed

### Movement Speed

| Comfort Stage | Movement Speed Change |
|---|---|
| Pain | **-20%** base movement speed |
| Normal | No change |
| Relax | **+20%** base movement speed |

### Digging Speed

| Comfort Stage | Digging Speed Change |
|---|---|
| Pain | **-10%** digging speed |
| Normal | No change |
| Relax | **+10%** digging speed |

---

## 📌The relationship between comfort and hunger consumption

Comfort also affects hunger consumption. It applies different basic consumption according to the player's state, and then multiplies it by a comfort multiplier.

At the same time, it will do additional hunger decay according to three state types: standing still, moving but not sprinting, and sprinting.

| Comfort Stage | Hunger Consumption Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Pain | × **1.5** |
| Normal | × 1.0 |
| Relax | × **0.5** |
