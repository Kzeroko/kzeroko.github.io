## Faction Entries

### Adventurer's Guild

A neutral organization spread across the continent of Urdas, not affiliated with any country, but following the ancient creed of freedom, contract, and strength. This is a gathering place for dreamers, wanderers, and eccentrics. Regardless of race or past experience, as long as you pass the assessment and obtain an adventurer's plaque, you are an adventurer. The Adventurer's Guild manages all paid commissions in the world, and as long as you have the strength, you can gain corresponding status and respect.

### The Shattered Dimension / Phantasmagoria

Unlike the stable reality of the Urdas continent, this is a chaotic realm that is detached from the main material plane. It is connected to the present world through randomly appearing spatial rifts, and the other side of the rifts is called a dungeon or a labyrinth. In the Phantasmagoria, the laws of physics are distorted and time no longer flows linearly. Adventurers may see a long-destroyed ancient country reappear in prosperity, or witness the tragic scene of the world's destruction hundreds of years later. It is full of illusion, impermanence, and fatal danger, but it also breeds rare materials that cannot be obtained from the outside world. For adventurers who are eager to get rich overnight, this is heaven, and for the weak, this is a grave without a tomb.

### Kingdom of Lanveris

Known as the Kingdom of Knights. Although the country is small, it has stood for hundreds of years with its treacherous terrain and noble knightly spirit. The kingdom advocates protection rather than aggression, and its core combat power, the White Rose Knights, is famous for its mastery of magic swordsmanship, and is a place of longing for all who aspire to become knights.

### The Clock Tower / Free City-State of Kaleidoscope

A free city built on a huge magitech device, and also a holy place in the hearts of magitech scholars all over the world. There are no laws here, only knowledge is the universal currency. In the center of the city stands a towering clock tower, and it is said that the greatest witch of the current generation lives at the top of the tower, constantly observing the changes in the world.

### The Sanctus

The most devout religious country on the continent, believing in the Supreme God. They have mastered top-notch healing magic and exorcism, and are the first line of defense against the invasion of the demon race. The people live under strict dogma. Although they are materially prosperous, they have a strong sense of exclusion towards outsiders and other races, especially the demon race and demi-humans.

### The Moonshadow Conclave

The elven regime hidden deep in the misty Eternal Forest. Due to their long lifespan and high sensitivity to magic, they are extremely exclusive and are committed to guarding the roots of the World Tree. Only a very small number of recognized adventurers or confidants are allowed to set foot in this ancient pure land.

### The Ashen Abyss

The Ashen Abyss is regarded as an absolute forbidden zone for life. The sky here is shrouded in a strange violet aurora all year round, which is a symbol of extreme spatial instability. In the center of the storm stands the demon capital, the Abyssal Citadel. In the legend, the Demon Lord Rhavaniel is here to summon an army of monsters from another dimension by tearing space with a forbidden secret technique, in an attempt to devour the entire continent of Urdas. For humans, this is a black cursed land on the map, but for the demon race, this is the last line of defense.

### Gracia Empire

In the chaotic era after the end of the age of gods, a powerful human hero united the fragmented kingdoms and established it. The history of the empire is a history of conquest and expansion. Today, the empire is the most powerful hegemonic country on the continent. The empire firmly believes that only absolute order and strong military power can guarantee the survival and prosperity of mankind. It has a pragmatic vigilance against other races, believing that those who are not of my race must have a different heart. The current empire is prosperous on the surface, but the nobles are corrupt inside, and the infiltration of the ████ cult is the most serious. Many high-ranking officials are secretly believers of the cult.

## Race Entries

### Demon

Demons have physical functions and magic adaptability far superior to humans. Although they are regarded as a symbol of disaster opposite to the gods due to historical reasons and have been exiled to the barren land of the far north, not all demons are full of hostility. In order to survive, many of them have learned to hide their characteristics and have quietly integrated into human society...

### Dragonkin

The dragonkin once ruled this continent as a superior race. The later dragonkin have lost the ability to transform into dragons, but they are still very excellent beings active in various areas... Just hearing their name can make the enemy tremble with fear.

### Dwarf

The craftsmanship of the dwarves is second to none on the continent of Urdas. Even if they are small, their strong bodies are enough to block the invasion of the enemy... Of course, if you want to be friends with them, bringing local fine wine is definitely a good idea.

### Elf

At the same time, they have excellent physical functions and supreme elemental affinity. Their ancestors have made achievements in the field of magic, and even have the great achievement of popularizing elven magic to this continent. It is said that they are a race blessed by the laws of nature and have some connection with the Feather Spirit race.

### Human

The more common inhabitants of the Urdas continent, humans do not have innate advantages, but they have a will to survive that is unmatched by all races. Influenced by various rumors, becoming a top adventurer is often a dream hidden in their hearts.

### Fox

Foxes are a relatively ancient existence. They are good at manipulating fire and soul, and can even operate celestial arts to a certain extent. So far, they have the responsibility of the ancient god's agent and live in seclusion from the world...

### Feline

Felines have light steps and keen intuition. They are not only excellent stealthers, but also swift fighters who can easily play with their opponents in close combat with their agile movements. It is said that their ancestors once made a contract with the god of shadow, so they obtained the ability to merge into darkness.

### Mecha

A powerful modified humanoid inorganic body left over from the previous generation. It was originally intended to be a guard unit to protect the ruins, but it has been sleeping for hundreds of years after being sealed in a certain event...

### Mermaid

They have absolute dominance in the water and are good at water magic. On land, they are no different from ordinary humans. Due to the protection of the sea god's essence, they will not be in danger of life due to lack of water...

### Oni

Oni have unparalleled strength and physical ability. Usually, oni are magic warriors, and their excellent physical enhancement ability is little known. It is said that they have some connection with the fox race...

### Rabbit Person

Rabbit people are born with excellent jumping ability and dexterity, and can easily travel through complex terrain, avoid danger and quickly counterattack. It is said that the rabbit people in ancient times had a special moonlight blessing, which gave them more excellent abilities at night.

### Slime

A humanoid creature with a peculiar body structure. Its ancestors could transform into slime, but the descendants could not transform due to long-term disguise, but they can still use most of the abilities of slime.

### Winged Spirit

A superior race that believes in gods, with certain flying ability, magic ability and healing ability. In fact, they are the descendants of the legendary angels. In ancient times, they were the right and left hands who directly assisted the gods. They who fell on the continent of Urdas have long forgotten their history...

## Magic/Sorcery Entries

In this world, almost all living beings have the ability to manipulate the flow of magic power, which is the law given to the world by the creator. The resonance phenomenon formed by the internal magic power of the body interfering with the external natural magic power is magic power manipulation. In general cognition, magic power manipulation cannot be performed without a medium, because it requires very high precision control for the magic power in the body to be directly materialized into a solid form, which is impossible to do when concentrating on gathering magic power.

### Magitech Conductor/Medium

At this time, the role of the medium is reflected. The medium is a conductor of magic power, used to guide and amplify magic power, so that the caster can cast magic and exert its maximum effect. Usually there are two types of magitech conductors: naturally formed magitech conductors and artificially forged magitech conductors. Among the naturally formed magitech conductors, the most well-known is the magitech crystal, a rare mineral located underground. It is rumored that several meteorites from outer space brought it to this continent in ancient times, and these crystals were gradually formed under long-term geological changes. With the development of the underground mining industry, the supply of natural magitech conductors is also approaching that of artificial magitech conductors at an amazing speed.

And the artificial magitech conductor is the embodiment of craftsmanship. By studying the wavelength of magic energy, craftsmen in ancient times smelted metal into a special alloy, and discovered the real formula through unremitting experiments, which was passed down from generation to generation, and became the magitech metal known to later generations. This skill is relatively mature in this era, and they are the most favorite materials for modern magicians and magic swordsmen to buy. Today, magitech metals are no longer produced to imitate natural magitech conductors, but to surpass them.

### Magician/Sorcerer

Magicians and sorcerers are professional groups that manipulate magic for combat and investigation. By chanting, they guide the power of nature and spirits to cause high magic damage to the enemy. Because it takes a long time to chant, magicians and sorcerers usually do not act alone. They are located in the back row of the team and become an indispensable role in the team by assisting their teammates and releasing supporting long-range output. Magicians and sorcerers are basically the same type of profession. The only small difference is that magicians use elemental magic, while sorcerers have the ability to use secret arts, such as light elemental magic and dark elemental magic, while manipulating elemental magic.

### Elemental Magic/Secret Arts

The elemental magics are fire, frost, water, wind, lightning, and earth. They are the most common magic elemental attributes. They may have different names in different regions, but the cognition of the six major elemental magics is common everywhere on the continent. Elemental magic is cast based on the power of the six major spirits/souls. Magicians and sorcerers borrow the corresponding elemental magic power by chanting to the micro-spirits everywhere. And the secret arts are to borrow power from the light/dark fairies. Because the power of the light and dark fairies is very dangerous, it requires considerable magic skills to control them, which is why sorcerers are high-level magicians.

### Chanting Magic/Power Guidance

Chanting magic also has a lot of knowledge. Generally, chanting is divided into low-level chanting and high-level chanting. Low-level chanting is to request and borrow power from a certain existence, and then transform it into one's own magic power to manifest; while high-level chanting is to give orders to a certain power, directly manipulate its power, and finally manifest it as magic. This also explains why high-level chanting is shorter than low-level chanting. The shorter the chanting, the stronger the bond with the power. The chanting time cannot be interfered with by external factors except for magitech tools. Magic without chanting is a rare existence.
