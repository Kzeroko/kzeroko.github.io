# KenToMahou2 🗡️Sword and Magic 2🌌 Modpack

The KTM2 Modpack, also known as Sword and Magic 2 Modpack, full name KTM Sword and Magic 2—The Chronicles Of Urdas, is a Minecraft RPG modpack currently under development by me ([@Kzeroko](https://space.bilibili.com/20218494)).

KTM2 includes over 250 mods, along with a series of custom modules called the "Isekai Series".
